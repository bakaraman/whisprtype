# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want WhisprType to be a respectful, technically sharp, and welcoming open source project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct
- Assume good intent
- Focus on the work, not the person
- Accept feedback without drama
- Keep disagreements concrete and technical

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or intimidation
- Personal attacks
- Bigoted language
- Deliberate derailing
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when needed to keep the project healthy.
